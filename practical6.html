Savitribai Phule Ji

A great social reformer who dedicated her life She strived to abolish discrimination and unfair treatment of people based on caste and gender. She and her husband were pioneers of women's education in India. They started their first school for girls in 1848 in Pune at Tatyasaheb Bhide's residence or Bhidewada. Bust of Savitribai Phule.
